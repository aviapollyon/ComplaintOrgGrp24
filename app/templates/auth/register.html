Republic of South Africa - POPIA (Act 4 of 2013)

DUT is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with POPIA. Before creating an account, please review and acknowledge the following:

What information we collect
  • Your full name and institutional email address
  • The content of complaints and grievances you submit
  • Any attachments uploaded in support of your complaint
  • Ticket activity history and communications with staff
Why we collect it
  • To process, manage, and resolve your complaint or grievance
  • To communicate ticket updates to you
  • To improve service delivery through analytics
How we protect it
  • Your data is stored securely on institutional servers
  • Access is restricted to authorized staff
  • Passwords are hashed and not stored in plain text
  • Data is not shared without consent unless required by law
Your rights under POPIA
  • You can request access to personal information held by DUT
  • You can request correction or deletion of your information
  • You can object to certain processing of your information
  • You can lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa
