Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) Consent

Republic of South Africa — POPIA (Act 4 of 2013)

The Durban University of Technology (DUT) is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA). Before you use the DUT Digital Grievance & Complaint Portal, please read and acknowledge the following:

What information we collect
  • Your full name and institutional email address
  • The content of complaints and grievances you submit
  • Any attachments you upload in support of your complaint
  • Ticket activity history and communications with staff
Why we collect it
  • To process, manage, and resolve your complaint or grievance
  • To communicate updates about your ticket to you
  • To improve institutional service delivery through analytics
How we protect it
  • Your data is stored securely on institutional servers
  • Access is limited to authorised staff only
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Your information will not be shared with third parties without your consent, except as required by law
Your rights under POPIA
  • You have the right to access your personal information held by DUT
  • You may request correction or deletion of your information
  • You may object to the processing of your information
  • You may lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa
By clicking "I Agree & Continue" you confirm that you have read, understood, and consent to the processing of your personal information as described above, for the purposes of the DUT Digital Grievance Portal.
